III. Project Description

1. Overview

a. Topic

Students can learn lectures online especially IT through our website, which is currently under construction. To learn the lesson, customers can access our website from any location and at any time using their devices such as cellphones, laptops, or tablets. Only by logging into their account on the website can students select lessons. As a result of this service, we hope to assist students in planning their educational path and improving their IT knowledge from a young age. Assigning homework is the only way for students to learn new chapters. Additionally, we have a team of consultants who are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to help students with their learning and plans. As technology advances, online learning services are becoming more and more important to students' educational needs. When customers use our services, they'll be fully supported by our team of professionals. Best educational solutions have always been our goal. Parental time and money are saved by using our service compared to traditional services. With mobile devices such as laptops and mobile phones, children and college students can also learn all subjects at any time. The number of IT training centers in Vietnam, such as Green Academy and Aptech FPT, is on the rise., and we hope to capture a portion of it so that the system can grow.

b. Motivation

Vietnamese like me recognize the need for additional education, especially in information technology, as well as the importance of continuing education. Information technology engineers are a serious shortage of human resources in Vietnam. Because of this, we aim to provide a service for online learning that meets the needs of today's learners. Parents send their children to private lessons in the majority of cases, according to a youth newspaper survey. A global trend toward online learning is emerging as a result of the Covid-19 pandemic. This site will hopefully help students acquire knowledge and become familiar with new learning methods instead of traditional ones.

2. Detailed Description

a. Aims

Acquiring knowledge has never been easier since the digitalization era. With the help of the Internet, tens of millions of people around the world have registered in Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) offered by many MOOC providers and other platforms. However, knowledge involving technology, in general, will require your brain to adapt cognitive frameworks and spend consistent time programming. In this project, we decided to develop a website called “TeachIT”. The platform will provide a learning ground for pupils to upload or view notes - harnessed through both internal and external resources conducted by other pupils, not from instructors. And the best part is, there’s no bar for expertise. Everyone can choose to upload something as little as tips for “making tools” or as huge as “developing hardware”. Hence, students are able to review what they learn in a lecture class and simplify those out-of-space coding exercises.

Programming doesn't need to be exhausting and every course permits you to implement your understanding into something live so that there's "learning" yet in addition "doing". This website is the ideal instance of "getting it going." It simply doesn't let you feel pressured with lots of courses, yet rather guides students to what is reasonable for themselves and cracking that afterward. Courses mostly come with an interactive community so that students can discuss the course and come up with solutions together. Regardless of doubts, problems, or a success to share, the community is a befitting place for that. Each of the courses comes with small assignments, and you get individual assistance from the support team. The subjects are constructed towards unlocking your succeeding career within the tech industry, determining how to solve business crises. They explain a wide range of concepts revolving around their vital focus, and the greatest part is, the courses are updated monthly.

Doing exercise after the lesson may not be enough. Therefore, the application should have a test function that students can check their understanding after finishing the exercises or after reading a lesson. Its purpose is to check how much the students can understand. It can be used after the lesson or exercises or every time when the students want to test themselves. It is necessary because the exercises may not be enough to check the understanding and they may need tests to see how much they have understood the course. Therefore, students are able to know what they are good at and what they need to improve.

The application will include a "Subscription" feature in which students (the administrators) can make a profit. Whenever we want to publish their works on the site, this function can be used to publish our products. There are many projects that can be produced and applied for any purpose while attending university, these projects can be creative and practical for daily life. Therefore, it would be unfortunate if these ideas or projects are skipped while they can be improved in quality. We can work on projects and receive experience dealing with clients about our works. This would also assist us in improving the quality of the applications, as we would be able to study and work at the same time while attending university. Aside from learning IT-related skills, we can gain business experience and learn to collaborate with a variety of people, all of which can benefit our future careers.

This is the individual that takes control of the platform and inspects the content. He or she has editorial rights over the content, the website, and manages the history of users involved in contributing to the website. Needless to say, the domain name is inseparable from the website so the administrator will register the name in one-year terms with a subscription fee deducted to pay for and can renew the domain as long as the budget is suffixed. Web Developer

The web developer takes charge of constructing the hardware and programming framework for the site. This contains the server is up 24/7 and guarantees stable internet connections to the server. The web engineer likewise regularly configures the web facilitating server like setting up email accounts, or changing the DNS, albeit at times the administrator would arrange these things themselves. Although An has the ambition to join the game development field, it shares some similar characteristics with the web Devs. Therefore, he will be assigned this role to set up basic tools (FTP, email server, web server, database, DNS, etc.).

f. Testing

In this project, the purpose is to create a learning site for IT students. Therefore, the website will synthesize knowledge about each of the courses and give users some exercises under each lesson. For that reason, the product needs to be tested by three groups. The first group will be us, the creators of the product will test how the product works and predict any errors that users could make. The second group will be the lecturers who will check the quality and also the accuracy of the lessons posted on the website and the assignments posted after each lesson. I believe checking the lesson's material should be done while the application is being developed. The information or the knowledge may not be absolute, but it should still be logical and students can read and understand it. This will also reduce the time for the second team to check the product. The final group will be the students, which is also the major user group of this application. Therefore, user testing is needed in this application. According to Jakob Nielson of user testing, we only need 3 to 5 participants from each group for user testing. When testing the users, 5 participants will give enough information about how the user would interact with the application and what kind of error they would make. Plan: There are multiple courses in this application, therefore, we need at most five users for user testing for each course. User testing will be done on the campus computer lab. Before the test takes place, there will be 2 team members who will be in charge of setting up the computers, checking the network connection, and instructing the participants about the test. There should be 2 users testing at the same time; the user testing should take 10 to 15 minutes for each person. We will give the users the lecture of their courses and ask them to finish a small exercise under the lesson to check their understanding and also how they interact with the user interface. The project will succeed if our application can run smoothly without any bugs. Secondly, we received good feedback from the user, and last, the website is able to be used widely by the students.
